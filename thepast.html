The Past

I've been in Dentistry my whole life...

I was always searching for something more, not sure what that something was, but I just knew I wasn't where I wanted to be in life.

Then on one sunny morning in January 2018, I was made redundant.

Don't feel bad for me. It was the best thing that they could have done! It made me sit and discover what it was that I actually wanted to do in life. It gave me the kick I needed to realise my potential.

Now I'm not saying the next year was easy - it was not.

I found part time work back at an old job, and after listening to yet another one of Brian Tracy's motivatonal talks on You Tube about goal setting in the summer, I took a piece of paper and wrote out exactly what I wanted to do and where I wanted to be in 5 years time.

I made a PLAN!

That afternoon, I enrolled with The Open University on their BSc in Cyber Security.

I'd encourage everyone to grab a sheet of paper right now, and write down your 5 year plan

What job are you in? How much do you earn? Where do you live? What car do you drive?

Write everything in the present tense - I am a Cyber Security Analyst. I drive a Tesla Model-S. That kind of thing.

It will amaze you, that just by writting it all down, the world will start to align and things will begin to happen faster than you imagined!

Make a commitment to looking at it as often as you can, and working on it every single day

I decided there and then that I was only going to concentrate on my future, and not sit and let another year pass me by without doing anything to get me to my goals

I know it sounds like a load of rubbish, but it really does work. You can't work towards changing your life if you don't know what kind of life you want!

I really hope this has inpired you to chase your dreams, as it is NEVER TOO LATE.
