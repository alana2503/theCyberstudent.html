The Future

In 2019 I signed up for a BSc in Cyber Security.

I've always loved Technology. To be honest I'm a bit of a geek when it comes to anything 'techy'

I am also obsessed with Space Exploration. I find it fascinating that we live on a Planet within a Solar System and yet there is so much more to discover out there. Who knows, maybe there is another Earth just like ours in a distant galaxy and they are also trying to make life multi-planetary. I am definately Team Elon when it comes to Space travel.

So where am I going?

Well, I knew I needed to get away from being a sales rep. With the invention of online shopping, that was a dying trade, and I knew eventually those roles would come to an end.

I knew I enjoyed computers, the tech field in general and also Space Exploration, so I wondered if there was something in which I could encorporate all of those into a career.

I have spent some time researching "dream jobs" and have a plan with what I need to study and achieve to get me there, including what qualifications they want, and qualities they look for etc. It's all in my Master Book!

I'm way off being in a position to apply for any of them anytime soon, but I feel I'll be in a strong position when I do.

I'll end with one of my favoruite quotes:

"Don't give up on a dream just because of the time it will take to accomplish it. The time will pass anyway"
